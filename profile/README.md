## üî¨ Research Focus

Our research employs **Community-Based Participatory Research (CBPR)** methodologies to:

- Document agrochemical exposure in agricultural workers
- Assess health impacts from pesticide use
- Develop machine learning models for health risk prediction
- Empower local communities through citizen science
- Promote evidence-based policy recommendations
